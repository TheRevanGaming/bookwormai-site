Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for writers, game devs, musicians, and lore hoarders.

Is Book Worm AI just another generic chatbot?

No. It’s tuned around canon, worldbuilding, and AAA-quality docs. The whole structure — tabs, modes, canon library, separate conversations — is built for big universes and multi-project creators.

Can I use it just for one book?

Absolutely. Even a single novel or novella benefits from having a central brain handling lore, character bios, world geography, magic/tech systems, and rewrite passes.

Does it replace human artists or voice actors?

No — it’s a tool. It helps you get organized, explore ideas, and prototype at AAA depth so that when you bring in artists, actors, or co-writers, you already have a rock-solid foundation.

Can I keep my worlds private?

Yes. Your canon lives inside your own projects and storage. The plan is to support both private worlds and optional shared/team worlds later.

Is this live production software?

Right now it’s an actively-developed creator tool, deployed for testing and personal use. Pricing links use Stripe test mode while features are evolving.

Can I suggest features?

Yes — this is built for real creators. Use the contact section below to share ideas, pain points, or dream features.
