Core Features

Built for creators who want more than a generic chatbot.

Worldbuilding Engine

From planetary geology and biomes to flora, fauna, cultures, and magic/tech systems — Book Worm helps you build worlds from the core outward, always respecting your canon.

Book & Storytelling Lab

Outline series, write chapters, deepen character arcs, and refine lore. Book Worm can keep track of your universe while you focus on the story you want to tell.

Game Dev Support

Design combat systems, progression, enemy archetypes, boss phases, missions, and game modes. Think of it as a game design partner built into your creative stack.

Language Lab

Create new languages (visual and spoken), naming conventions, scripts, and linguistic rules that feel consistent and believable across your world.

Image & Concept Support

Turn your descriptions into powerful prompts for high-quality concept art and reference images to help visualize characters, locations, and key scenes.

Canon Memory (Per Project)

Projects stay isolated so your epic fantasy doesn’t bleed into your sci-fi horror. Each project has its own canon, notes, and creative history.
