FAQ

Is this really just one person’s project?

Yes. Book Worm AI is being built by a single creator who’s obsessed with worldbuilding, game design, and storytelling. Early access helps fund further development.

What do I need to use it?

A modern browser, an internet connection, and a Stripe-supported payment method if you decide to subscribe after any trial period.

Does the AI own my IP?

No. The worlds, characters, and stories you create are yours. Treat Book Worm AI like a studio assistant, not a rights-holder.

Can it generate actual images and audio?

It specializes in extremely detailed prompts, design docs, lyrics, and creative direction. Actual rendering depends on your connected tools and API access.
