Beta FAQ

Is this a finished product?

No. Book Worm AI is an evolving tool in active development. Features may change, break, and improve rapidly. You’re getting in early.

Where does the AI actually run?

The AI core runs on a dedicated backend (currently hosted via Render) using OpenAI models under the hood, wrapped in a custom brain designed specifically for your worlds and projects.

Do I need to log in to use it?

For now, accounts are stored per browser and per device. That will eventually expand into full multi-device, cloud-backed accounts.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. All subscriptions are handled through Stripe. You can pause or cancel anytime via your Stripe customer portal.
